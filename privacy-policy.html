Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Data Collection

This extension collects anonymous usage data to improve user experience:

  • Extension installation events
  • Banner display events
  • Redirect success events (without article URLs)
  • Learn More button clicks

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No personal information, browsing history, or article content is collected or transmitted.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics. Google may process data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The "Learn More" button links to an external community (Skool). This link opens in a new tab, and we do not track any activity on that website.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
